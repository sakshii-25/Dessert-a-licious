Fruit cake is a traditional dessert beloved for its rich, dense texture and sweet, fruity flavor. It typically consists of a moist sponge cake base infused with a variety of dried fruits such as raisins, currants, chopped dates, and candied citrus peel. Often enjoyed during festive seasons like Christmas or as a tea-time treat, fruit cake is known for its long shelf life, owing to the preserving qualities of the dried fruits and sometimes alcohol content. It's often adorned with a layer of marzipan or icing, adding a touch of sweetness to its already indulgent taste. Whether served as a slice with a cup of tea or as a centerpiece dessert at celebrations, fruit cake remains a timeless classic in many culinary traditions.
